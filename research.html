Research

 

 

My research interest is to experimentally study the ultrafast dynamics of atoms and molecules.Initially my research goal at IITH is to develop a tabletop coherent soft X-ray source and combine it with a 4pi-solid-angle-acceptance coincident electron-ion momentum imaging spectrometer in order to study photon-induced dynamics in small and large molecules in real time.
