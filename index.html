About CT

My name is Chengappa Thumisi born and brought up in Pathikonda, Chittoor District in Andhra Pradesh State in India.

I completed the primary education in M.P.U.P.School in Pathikonda and got secondary School Certificate from Board of Secondary Education Andhra Pradesh.

Then, I completed the Intermediate Education and got the certificate from Board of Intermediate Education Andhra Pradesh.

I have done my graduation in Physics, Mathematics and Chemistry from Sri Venkateswara University Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh. During this time, in addition to this course I have done the Post Graduation Diploma in Computer Applications.

I have done my Post  Graduation in Physics with the specialization "Condensed Matter Physics" from Sri Venkateswara University Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh.

At present, I am working in Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad as a Technical staff member in Department of Physics.

Professional Experience
